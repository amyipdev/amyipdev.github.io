# Song Reviews

Here are reviews of some of my favorite artists' songs. They're sorted by artist, with more recently reviewed artists and songs being relative at the bottom.

## girl in red

### Serotonin

Serotonin is easiily one of the best songs I've ever listened to. Unlike the majority of girl in red's songs, it's not about being queer, but rather it addresses intrusive thoughts, something she personally has suffered from. It also expands beyond her normal genre and style of music to create something highly enjoyable and that can be listened to on repeat. It works in almost any scenario - whether that be a workout song, a quick pick-me-up when emotions are turning sour, and more. At surface level, it's already an extremely incredible song, but looking at the lyrics helps reveal her thoughts which led to writing this song. I highly recommend listening to this song and adding it to playlists; it's one of the only songs I've listened to in the past few days from writing this.

**Spotify link**: [Listen](https://open.spotify.com/track/1q2Zb0v5ZGxKDktEz7wSPb?si=jrlxYBaIS_ul5pbats_V1Q)

**Reviewed on**: March 8, 2021

**Rating**: 10/10

---

### two queens in a king sized bed

We got a bit of an early Christmas present with two queens in a king sized bed. I'm not super into Christmas music, but it's one of the best Christmas songs I've ever listened to. Once again, girl in red impresses us all with her incredible singing and lyric writing. I'm not quite sure how to comment on it; it's simply excellent.

**Spotify link**: [Listen](https://open.spotify.com/track/1pUyZuge2qoSG1nzDxftQJ?si=YqQgryrOTNaQVCX0jLuCEg)

**Reviewed on**: March 8, 2021

**Rating**: 9/10

---

### rue

Rue is an excellent song, and a great addition to girl in red's first full sized album. The background music is incredible, and the lyrics are spectacular. It's extremely catchy and will have you memorizing the lyrics in no time. It also fits with almost anything; you can slot it into any situation. While it's no where near as good as Serotonin, it's still incredible, and highly deserving of a 10 rating.

**Spotify link**: [Listen](https://open.spotify.com/track/6lhuBaygBqxBjdcXrM685k?si=hvGlNlafSr6zpnAts0vcUg)

**Reviewed on**: March 8, 2021

**Rating**: 10/10

---

### Say It

Spotify made an excellent choice by having girl in red lead the new Norwegian Studio Oyster. Say It is an incredible song, and I'm glad girl in red got the time to perform there. It's a nice, safe, romantic song you can listen to anywhere. It's almost as catchy as rue, and the background music is a great pick-me-up. The lyrics are a bit lackluster, however; and the song goes at an odd pace without as thorough of a plot-line as girl in red's other songs. Nevertheless, it's still a great song, and can be binged on repeat for hours.

**Spotify link**: [Listen](https://open.spotify.com/track/4677jRCDMa05jcA94EQ0hG?si=ja66vsaoQgm9GroJ3QgzTA)

**Reviewed on**: March 8, 2021

**Rating**: 8/10
