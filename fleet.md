## Desktops and Servers

### Samantha

Samantha is my primary computer for day-to-day at-home work. It was first built in 2018 and has endured many upgrades since.

- **CPU**: AMD Ryzen 7 3700X
- **Cooler**: Corsair H100i PRO Hydro Series
- **RAM**: 2x16 [32GB] CL16 DDR4-3200 TeamGroup (Hynix)
- **Case**: NZXT H510i
- **GPU**: Gigabyte ITX OC GTX 1070
- **Motherboard**: MSI B450 Tomahawk MAX
- **NVMe SSD**: WD SN750 1TB
- **OS**: Arch Linux

### Sappho

Sappho is my primary server which does my heavy lifting. It acts as a test bed for software, and is comprised primarily of former Samantha components.

- **CPU**: AMD Ryzen 7 1700X
- **Cooler**: Wraith Prism
- **RAM**: 2x8 [16GB] CL16 DDR4-2400 Crucial Ballistix 
- **GPU**: MSI Low-profile GT 710
- **Motherboard**: MSI B350M Gaming Pro
- **HDD**: Toshiba P300 1TB
- **OS**: Rotating (Ubuntu Server 20.04 LTS, RHEL 8.3, or FreeBSD)
